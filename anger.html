Clinical Anger Scale

The group of items below inquire about the types of feelings you have. Each of the 21 groups of items has four options. The Clinical Anger Scale (CAS) is used by healthcare professionals to estimate the degree of Clinical Anger (Chronic and pervasive anger). Clinical anger differs from the common emotion in terms of the distress caused to the individual and the time period for which the person has experienced the emotion.
